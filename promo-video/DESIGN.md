## Style Prompt

Create a calm, credible NHS staff promotional video for the Tees Neurodevelopmental Symposium. The visual language follows the website: white and ice-blue clinical space, NHS blue accents, cyan network lines and brief travelling light pulses. The mood is practical, professional and polished, not salesy.

## Colors

- Background: #f4fbff and #ffffff
- Primary ink: #061b31
- NHS blue: #005eb8
- Deep NHS blue: #003087
- Cyan accent: #00a9ce
- Soft line: #d7e8f7

## Typography

- Headline: readable system serif, bold, high contrast.
- Interface and details: system sans-serif.

## Motion

- Slow ambient network movement in the background.
- Topic cards enter in small cascades.
- Light flashes travel along network lines.
- Smooth fades and short vertical lifts; no harsh spins or bouncy motion.

## What NOT To Do

- No patient imagery, no placeholder faculty photos, no cartoon medical graphics.
- No dark nightclub/neon treatment.
- No crowded walls of full talk titles.
- No decorative blobs that obscure text.

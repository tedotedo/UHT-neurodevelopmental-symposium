Previous symposium pattern

A practical educational series with national expert input.

Previous programmes show a consistent focus on clinically useful learning for paediatricians, nurses and therapists.

2023

4th Neurodevelopmental Paediatric e‑Conference

Equity and inclusion, rare neurogenetic disorders, palliative care, CAMHS interface, immunology and child protection.

2024

5th Neurodevelopmental Paediatric e‑Conference

Dystonia, NF1, cerebral palsy, physiotherapy and the role of orthopaedic surgery in improving function.

2025

6th Neurodevelopmental Paediatric e‑Conference

Anxiety in autistic children, the genetics of autism, safeguarding, seizure mimics, whole genome sequencing and bowel issues.

2026

15th Tees Symposium

Expanded two-day face-to-face programme bringing together neurodevelopmental and neurological practice.
